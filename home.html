About MickeyBee

My name is McCall Butler otherwise known as Mickey Bee or MickeyBeeMakeup, and I have been a makeup artist for 4 years. I am MAC certified and am from Houston, Texas but am currently freelancing out of Utah. Everyday I feel so blessed and fortunate that I get to continue to explore and pursue my passion in doing something I love so much. Although makeup is my medium, what I enjoy most in this business is getting to meet amazing people and helping them to look and feel like the best version of themselves.Confidence is key in life and when you have a good makeover, there is not much that can get in your way.There is nothing more gratifying to me than seeing both men and woman feel confident in who they are. Life is short, so why not feel beautiful? Let me help bring out the beauty in you . - xoxo MickeyBee ​
